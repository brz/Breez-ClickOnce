Information before using Breez ClickOnce

The Breez ClickOnce extension uses native messaging technology to launch the ClickOnce application.
This means that whenever you are trying to start a ClickOnce application, the ClickOnce application's URL is being sent to the Breez ClickOnce helper application.
Please note that this happens inside the context of your local computer.
No data is being sent to external parties and your privacy is safe, but we need your consent in order to activate this extension.

Refer to the privacy policy for more information.
